Privacy Policy


Basic or Bizarre was developed by Prachi Heda as an open source app powered by the Spotify Web API. This service is not intended for monetization. By choosing to use this app, you agree to the collection and use of information with relation to this policy.

The information collected from the user is limited to their username, playlist information and playlist track information related to the link inputted (only for public playlists). None of the aforementioned data is used for any purpose apart from calculating a uniqueness score for the playlist. No information shared will be stored, used, or shared with any third parties.

For any further inquiries or suggestions, please contact ppheda@ucsd.edu.

Thanks for using Basic or Bizarre!
